WHAT IS THE FOCUS OF THIS SCHOOL?




PSHS-CRC focuses on STEM education. It is focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics where in students are only allowed to choose their careers related to STEM. This is centered to the improvement of its educational learning with science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) education.

IS PSHS-CRC AN IDEAL SCHOOL?

Yes. PSHS-CRC is a recommended school for high school students who want to be national leaders or professionals in the future. It is an excellent choice to apply for an admission at this school because it provides numerous benefits, the most notable of which is the advancement and improvement of each student's skills and intellect. PSHS-CRC would be a great pick!

WHAT DOES THE PHILIPPINE SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL - CARAGA REGION CAMPUS OFFERS?

EDUCATION & ENVIRONMENT
It offers high-quality, advanced education in an enjoyable and friendly setting for students. Being a PSHS scholar is a fantastic opportunity since it provides students with an efficient study environment and a supportive community that will benefit them in the long run. Compliance with regulatory regulations will foster their young minds while also training and improving their abilities.

ALLOWANCE
It additionally offers students with a monthly stipend because every student at Philippine Science High School/ 'PISAY' is a scholar. The stipend for each student may vary depending on a variety of factors, including but not limited to the gross of the parent's pay.

CORES & CAREERS
Specialties and elective courses are available to senior high school students at PSHS-CRC. Students in their senior year of high school have the option of selecting their favorite core—biology, physics, or chemistry—which will subsequently allow them to select a career connected to their selected or assigned core.

FLAG CEREMONY
Every Monday, the PSHS-CRC conducts flag ceremonies as an official means to usher in a new week. Every week, all faculty, staff, and students attend the flag ceremony. Usually, there will be announcements from the SSD, CID, and/or FAD in addition to the campus director's remarks.

PHYSICAL ACTIVITIES
Students are free to play in designated areas of the campus, including Stem Park, the gym, the grandstand, the oval, and the front of Academic Building 1 (see picture).

FEILD TRIPS
Students at PSHS-CRC get to go on enjoyable and unforgettable field trips and take some school bus rides. The school bus is mostly utilized for school-related official business, but it can also be used for field trips and other official or non-campus activities with permission from the PSHS-CRC authorities.

SANGGUNIANG ISKOLAR
The student council takes the lead in advancing, improving, and bettering the PSHS-CRC community and environment. They advocate for a better environment for the community.

TREE PLANTINGS
In an effort to improve and enhance the campus, the PSHS-CRC organizes clean-up drives, tree plantings, and many other events. Sir Ramil A. Sanchez, the director of the CRC campus, encourages and supports the GPTA, faculty, staff, students, and the PSHS-CRC community at large to get involved and make a positive impact on the campus' shape and potential as well as the well-being of the surrounding area and its people.

CARD GIVING
On a quarterly basis, the Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus hosts awards and recognitions. Here is the opportunity to show students, whether or not their names are on the director's list, how much they are valued and worthy of their efforts because they worked hard to achieve this positive juncture.

DOES PSHS-CRC PROVIDES ENOUGH RESOURCES?

Yes, absolutely! PSHS-CRC has adequate tools, including basic laboratory materials that granted students to conduct scientific laboratory experiments and activities under the supervision of laboratory advisers/teachers, as well as high-quality tools for senior high-grade levels that may be utilized for research and other activities related to their chosen cores. To add,  CRC has superior laboratory equipment that other high schools might lack, such as the FTIR and UV-vis.

WHAT MAKES THE PHILIPPINE SCIENCE HIGHSCHOOL STANDOUT AMONG OTHER SCHOOLS?

Philippine Science High School Campuses, particularly PISAY CARAGA, feature expert faculties who are beyond great at helping and mentoring pupils strengthen and improve their abilities, utilities that keep the surroundings clean, guards that ensure campus safety, and, of course, the entire community. This community promotes the idea that everyone should be involved and that no one should be left behind, regardless of their differences. This school encourages students to become more active individuals, since when they converse and interact with one another on campus, their social skills increase. PSHS-CRC is about more than simply the consistent objective of most of us—advancement and progress for all—it is also about the unforgettable experiences, friendships formed, connections made with others, and lasting memories created on this campus.
